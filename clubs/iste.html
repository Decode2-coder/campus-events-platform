What is ISTE?

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) stands as a beacon of educational excellence, fostering a culture of innovation, learning, and professional growth in the realm of technical education. Through its multifaceted initiatives and programs, ISTE creates a dynamic platform for educators, students, and industry professionals to exchange knowledge, ideas, and best practices, thus enriching the academic ecosystem with a tapestry of insights and perspectives. With its flagship annual intercollegiate festival, ISTE orchestrates a captivating symphony of talent, creativity, and academic prowess, serving as a vibrant conduit for students and faculty members from diverse institutions to showcase their skills, participate in intellectually stimulating competitions, and collaborate on cutting-edge research and technological innovations.
