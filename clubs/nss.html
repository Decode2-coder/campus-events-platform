What is NSS?

At Vidyalankar Institute of Technology, our National Service Scheme (NSS) represents unity and leadership within our student community. As a unit of 100, NSS at VIT fosters strong bonds among students and instills essential life skills like teamwork and communication. Our students actively engage in various community service initiatives, from orphanage teaching to tree planting drives, beach cleanup drives and developing a strong sense of citizenship.

NSS at VIT goes beyond volunteerism; it transforms our students into responsible and compassionate leaders. Through this program, they learn to appreciate diversity and the importance of national integration.
